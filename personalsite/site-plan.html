Overview

Purpose

Dungeons & Dragons is probably the biggest TTRPG (Table Top Role Playing Game) out there. For those who are new to RPG's, and especially to D&D, it can be daunting to decide what to do and where to start! What do you need to start? How exactly does the game work? If you've wondered something like that before, then wonder no more! As someone who has played the game for many years, I know quite a bit about those questions! This website will have those answers in a convenient location, and might even answer some questions you didn't know you had!

Audience

You! You are my audience! If you are wondering what could be so great about Dungeons and Dragons, then what I've got here is specifically for you!

Content

Home page

People will come to Is D&D For M&E because it will provide them a concise and informative location to learn more about the largest and most popular Roleplaying Game in the world! Getting started, and learning what that would entail can be a daunting task, and this website will make those fun and easy! The information here will answer questions such as "What do I actually do in Dungeons and Dragons?", "How do I make my first Character?", "What options do I have for my character?", and many others.

On this site, you will be able to see a brief overview of not only Dungeons and Dragons, but also a brief overview of the available official character options, and class options. Along with the information for each of those topics will be a picture as well to give you a feel for what that might look like. Ever wondered what a Half-Orc Ranger would look like? Or maybe you want to know what a Paladin does, either way, you're in the right place! If these are things that you want to learn more about, then enough wasting time, lets find out what its all about together!

Classes List

Now for the fun and exciting part! Learning about what classes are available for you as a player in Dungeons and Dragons! But, what is a class? Well, you will learn that on this page! Go there to find out what a class is, what classes you can choose from, and what each of those classes can do. Along with that information, there will also be recommended character options, caharacter background ideas, and the strengths and weaknesses of choosing that class. The goal is to provide you with all the information you would need when selecting something as important as who your character is in Dungeons and Dragons. A class is a pretty big decision in Character Creation, so it's important that you are properly aware of what your choice will mean. Of course, really any class is fun, and there is no objective class that is better than any other one, but each person who plays Dungeons and Dragons gravitates towards a particular playstyle, and finding yours earlier is better! Of course, now that you know what this page is going to be about, the only thing left for you to do is leave this short synopsis paragraph behind you and go explore all the options that you can find for creating your first of many D&D characters!

Character Options

So you know you want to play Dungeons and Dragons, and you have an idea for what class or profession you want for your character. What's next? Well, the next thing for you is going to be determining what type of character you want to play! This page is all about who you will be, are you a Dwarf? an Elf? maybe youre a Dragonborn! No matter what you think you might choose, all the official character options will be listed here for your choice and for your convenience. Each character background option comes with its own perks and drawbacks. Some of them are very common, and the normal everyday folk would noot bat an eye in the typical Dungeons and Dragons game. But some of them are uncommon or even rare, and its important to know what the NPCs or Non Player Characters of the D&D game would think about your character. Some of them are monstrous or even dangerous in nature, and before picking that background it would help you to know how to interact with roleplaying encounters you find yourself in. All of these things will be found within the Character Options page here. So with all that said, what is that you're waiting for? Go on and find out which background speaks to you!
